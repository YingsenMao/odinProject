Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked pasta dish consisting of layers of wide, flat pasta (lasagna noodles) alternating with fillings like ragù (meat sauce), béchamel (white sauce), and various cheeses, often including ricotta and mozzarella, baked until bubbly and golden.
